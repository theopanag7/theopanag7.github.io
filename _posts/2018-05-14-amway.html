During the Summer of 2018 I interned with Amway's R&D, working on mobile Augmented Reality (AR) applications.

My job was to explore Augmented Reality as a technology and find possible use cases for Amway. To that end, I designed and developed 2 AR prototypes focused on interactive packaging.

My goal was to enhance the Amway experience by adding a digital extension to Amway packages. I worked on both the social/promotional aspect of Augmented Reality as well as the immersive/entertaining one.

The internship involved a final presentation, where I pitched my work and its importance to approximately 100 Amway employees (management, marketing, etc.). The presentation included live demos of the prototypes and an extensive Q&A session. The project was received very well.

Tools


I developed 2 iOS applications, using Obj-C (not Swift, because of library limitations).

The AR applications were built on the Vuforia AR library, and on a combination of OpenGL ES & Scenekit for 3D rendering. Additional tools like Blender were also utilized.
